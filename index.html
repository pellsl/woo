Are You an Explorer?

Imagine yourself adrift on an ocean far from home. Maybe you see a deserted island, and you paddle frantically for it. Upon reaching those restive shores, that island sanctuary, you take a moment to consider your surroundings. You see a stream, so you have a water source. Palm trees ring the beach, with coconuts aplenty. The lush inner jungle is heavy with fruit. "Is this Eden?" you ask yourself. And then you remember the fish that you saw while paddling yourself toward the island. You recall the harmonious sounds and the vibrant colors of the Trumpetfish heard from just below the diamond-studded waves.

The Trumpetfish is a most noble creature. One that is strangled by humanity. Its haunting beauty is the Sirens' Song of the sea. The International Trumpetfish Advocacy, Recovery and Tracking Society is committed to assuring the survival and proliferation of this beautiful animal, so that future generations might benefit and learn from its profound impact on the oceanic community.

We hope that you might hear the plight of this enigmatic creature and lend your hand in assisting I.T.F.A.R.T.S. in locating and identifying Trumpetfish globally.
